— BIO

Marcus Maldonado is a Texas-based guitarist with 15+ years on stage. By day he writes software; by night he plays lead for country artist Don Louis and performs at private weddings, corporate events, and international engagements through Aura Entertainment. Recent stops include the ACM Kickoff in Frisco, a festival headline in Hawaii, and a ten-day run in Greece.
