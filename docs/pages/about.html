— BIO

Engineer by trade. Guitarist by passion.
Equally serious about both.

Marcus has spent 12 years doing two things seriously: writing software and playing guitar. While most people pick one lane, he context-switches between them daily — thriving in both analytical and creative environments. Parallel to his successful corporate career in tech, his time in the music industry has taken him places he never dreamed of. His musical approach has landed him incredible opportunities both domestic and international, as he's been able to perform on festival stages across the country, headline engagements abroad, and open for artists he grew up listening to. He currently plays lead guitar for the breakout country artist Don Louis, performs at luxury events with AURA, and plays guitar at multiple local churches in DFW.
